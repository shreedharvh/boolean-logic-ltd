90-day working rhythm

What happens after you enquire
Week 1

Audit the current funnel

We review your offer, service areas, website journey, analytics setup, ad accounts, search demand, and what a valuable enquiry means for your business.

Weeks 2-3

Fix tracking and launch priorities

We agree the first campaign sprint, set up the key conversion events, clean up obvious landing-page friction, and define what will be reported weekly.

Weeks 4-8

Optimize against real enquiries

We adjust search terms, budgets, ad copy, landing-page messaging, and content priorities based on the quality of leads coming through.

Weeks 9-12

Decide the next sprint

You get a plain-English review of what changed, what worked, what did not, and whether the next best move is scale, repair, or a different channel.
